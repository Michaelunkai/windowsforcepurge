### Apache Spark
**Apache Spark: Explained**
Apache Spark is an open-source unified analytics engine designed for large-scale data processing. It is known for its speed, ease of use, and sophisticated analytics. Spark provides high-level APIs in Java, Scala, Python, and R, and includes built-in modules for streaming, SQL, machine learning, and graph processing. Its in-memory computation capabilities significantly speed up data processing tasks, making it suitable for big data analytics. Spark is highly versatile, supporting complex workflows and iterative algorithms. It integrates well with Hadoop and other big data tools, making it a preferred choice for data engineers and data scientists working on large datasets.
